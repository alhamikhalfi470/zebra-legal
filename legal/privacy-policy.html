Privacy Policy for Zebraa

Effective date: July 2, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Zebraa ("Zebraa", "we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use the Zebraa iOS app. Zebraa is a visual research and moodboard app that lets you browse public Are.na channels and blocks, save permitted references locally, and use Zebraa Pro features.

If you have questions, contact us at kicikkustudio@gmail.com.

Zebraa is owned by kicikkustudio@gmail.com.

Summary

Zebraa does not sell personal data, does not show third-party advertising, and does not track you across apps or websites. Zebraa does not operate its own user account system. Most Zebraa library data is stored locally on your device.

Information Zebraa Handles

Public Are.na Content

Zebraa uses the Are.na API to display public channels, blocks, users, images, embeds, source links, and related metadata. When you browse or search in Zebraa, your device sends requests to Are.na and may also load media from Are.na or from third-party source URLs referenced by Are.na content.

The content and account information shown from Are.na is controlled by Are.na and the relevant content owners, not by Zebraa.

Search Queries and Browsing Requests

When you search for channels, blocks, or users, the search text and related request details are sent from your device to Are.na so Are.na can return results. Zebraa does not separately store your search queries on our own servers.

Local Library Data

Zebraa can store the following data locally on your device:

  • Saved Are.na blocks and channels
  • Bookmarked channels and custom folders
  • Local reference files that you choose to save
  • Export drafts and generated moodboard/contact sheet output
  • App preferences such as theme settings
  • Zebraa Pro access status and product identifiers

This local data is stored on your device using app storage, local files, and local databases. Zebraa does not upload your local library to our servers.

Zebraa may also store Zebraa Pro entitlement status, product identifiers, and transaction identifiers in local app preferences and Apple's iCloud key-value storage so your Pro access can be restored across your Apple devices.

Photos Access

If you choose to save images to your photo library or choose images from your photo library, iOS will ask for permission. Zebraa uses photo library access only to perform the action you requested, such as saving selected images or allowing you to choose media.

Purchases and Zebraa Pro

Zebraa Pro purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. Apple handles payment information, billing, refunds, and subscription management. Zebraa receives product identifiers, transaction identifiers, and entitlement status from StoreKit so the app can unlock Zebraa Pro features. Zebraa does not receive your full payment card details.

Support and Content Reports

If you contact support or report content, Zebraa may open an email draft. If you send that email, we receive the email address, message content, and any details you choose to include. We use support emails only to respond to your request, investigate reports, and improve the app.

Third Parties

Zebraa relies on the following third parties:

  • Apple, for App Store distribution, StoreKit purchases, subscription management, refunds, and iOS platform services.
  • Are.na, for public content, search results, channels, blocks, users, and related API responses.
  • Third-party websites or media hosts, when Zebraa loads source URLs or media referenced by Are.na content.

Those services may process information according to their own privacy policies. Zebraa is not responsible for third-party services that we do not control.

Tracking and Advertising

Zebraa does not use your data to track you across apps or websites owned by other companies. Zebraa does not include third-party advertising SDKs.

Data Retention

Local library data remains on your device until you delete it in the app, clear relevant app data, or uninstall Zebraa. Local reference files and exports remain on your device until deleted by you.

Support emails are retained only as long as reasonably necessary to respond to you, maintain support records, resolve disputes, and comply with legal obligations.

Purchase history and subscription records are managed by Apple according to Apple's policies.

Deleting Your Data

You can delete local Zebraa data by deleting saved items in the app or uninstalling the app from your device. To request deletion of support emails or other information you sent directly to us, contact kicikkustudio@gmail.com.

If you have an Are.na account or want to delete content hosted by Are.na, you must manage that content through Are.na.

Children's Privacy

Zebraa is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has sent us personal information, contact us and we will take appropriate steps to delete it.

International Use

Zebraa may be used in different countries. Third-party services such as Apple, Are.na, email providers, and media hosts may process information in countries outside your location.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the effective date and make the revised policy available through the app or the public policy URL.
